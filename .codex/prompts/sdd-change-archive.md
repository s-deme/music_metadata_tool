# SDD Change Archive Command

Archive a completed or rejected change proposal.

## Process

1. Confirm the proposal status from code, specs, and tests.
2. Move or summarize it under `storage/changes/<change-name>/`.
3. Record whether it was completed, superseded, or rejected.
4. Link the final implementation or reason it was not applied.

## Rules

- Keep the archive factual and short.
- Do not create fictional migration steps or release notes.
